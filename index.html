CAD
Our CAD process consists of 3 key steps. Conceptualize, act, and deviate. Our first step is to conceptualize ideas and illustrate them to our teammates. This way, we can collaborate on a common idea and work towards designing it one part at a time. Next, we act upon our concept and form it using a 3D modeling software, or in other words a CAD software. Finally, we deviate from our original concept to explore other options and constantly innovate. Our current design for our robots function consists of a ramp and a slope. The ramp, beautifully designed by our hard-working CADers, is nearly level with the floor making it easy for our robot to pick up the scoring elements this season. Our slope (under constant tweaking) is laid by the foundation of an angle that curves upward. On the sides of the slope, we utilized “pocketing”, a certain method that allows for easier management of cables within our robot. Overall, our CAD team and the designs they created laid the foundation for our robots function in this years game.
